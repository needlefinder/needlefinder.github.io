Zhongxu is a student in the Department of Electronic Engineering of Tsinghua University. He joined the SPL for the summer 2016. He investigated methods to automatically detect in MRI data the tips of the catheters used during the brachytherapy procedures. After exploring conventional image-based filters, he had great success in implementing a Convolutional Neural Network.
