Directions

First, follow directions below to the Brigham and Women's Hospital. BWH is located between Huntington and Brookline Avenues, within the Longwood medical and academic area. Once you are inside the Brigham's main lobby, take the Amory elevators across from the information desk down to L1. When you leave the elevators, turn right. Walk down the hallway: the third door on your right is marked SPL. Enter the reception area. (Access outside of hospital business and visiting hours requires BWH identification.)
For more information, please visit: http://www.spl.harvard.edu/pages/Directions#Getting_to_the_SPL
